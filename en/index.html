About Fair Trade Compass

Fair Trade Compass is an independent bilingual media outlet covering the fair trade ecosystem — from the farms that grow our coffee, cocoa and cotton, to the certification bodies and policies that shape the market. We publish daily in English and Japanese with the goal of making ethical sourcing easier to understand for buyers, businesses, and curious readers alike.
